Aurora Vanguard

A stalwart alliance, the Aurora Vanguard stands as a beacon of hope in a world veiled by darkness. Masters of light and valor, they safeguard ancient wisdom and protect the realms from encroaching shadows.

Umbral Covenant

A clandestine faction steeped in shadow and secrecy, the Umbral Covenant delves into forbidden magics and ancient rites. Veiled in mystery, they pursue arcane knowledge hidden from the light, their allegiance known only to those who dare seek the shadows.
